Solitaire

Posted on Oct 27, 2023.

Set up

Set up 7 columns, from left to right:

  1. card
  2. cards
  3. cards
  4. cards
  5. cards
  6. cards
  7. cards








The topmost card on each one is the only card facing up

Your card stack will be your draw pile. Set a designated discrad pile area too.

You will have 4 foundation stacks where you can place 1 card and its descendants.

How to play

Visible cards can be transferred from the stock pile or the columns to the foundation stacks or other columns. When moving a card to a column, it must be of a lower rank and have a different color. For instance, if the card is a 9 of hearts (red), you can place an 8 of spades or clubs on top of it. Stacks of cards can be moved from one column to another as long as they maintain the same order (highest to lowest, alternating colors). If you have an empty column, you can start a new column with a King. To obtain new cards from the stock pile, turn three cards at a time face up into the waist stack next to the stock pile. Only the top card of the waist stack can be played. If you run out of stock cards, flip the waist stack over to create a new stock pile and begin again by taking the top three cards, turning them over, and creating a new waist stack.

Memory Game

Posted Nov 03, 2023.

Set up

This one's simple. Place all the cards you have face down in whatever order you want!

How to play

  • Start flipping them two at a time.
  • If your pictures match: Congrats!
  • Put them aside and keep going.
  • The game is over when all pictures are found.
