Hive Pocket

Posted on Aug 13, 2023.

Set up

There are 11 black and white pieces. Each player picks a color.

Here's the meaning of each:

  • Queen Bee: can move 1 space adjecently
  • Spider: can move 3 spaces adjecently
  • Grasshopper: can hop any pieces in a straight line as long as they connect
  • Soldier Ant:can move adjecently to any pieces
  • Beetle: only moves 1 space, but can climb on other pieces; the piece underneath takes its color and can't be moved; unlimited beetles can be stacked

How to play

The goal of the game is to enclose the opponent's queen in the hive.

Taking turns, you start placing pieces. By the 4th turn, the queen must be put on in the game.

You can't break the hive.
